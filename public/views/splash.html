Topsoil's mission is to create a community of people who want to grow good food. Have you always wanted a garden but don't have the oomph it takes to get going on one? Or perhaps you are that gardener, but are lacking the resources/land to grow your goods on... Either way, we're here to help! Because who doesn't want to make this urban world a little greener?
